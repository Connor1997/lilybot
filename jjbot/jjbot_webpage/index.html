This is your dashboard to your for your lilybots. Load the lilybot client onto your Raspberry Pi. It will automatically connect to the ardyh server. You can control the lilybot using the keypad or using your keyboard. The keys are listed in paranthesis on the keypad.

In the log you can see commands issued from you to your lilybot.
